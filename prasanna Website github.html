New Calvary Ministries

In 2004, after years of dedicated prayer and preparation, Paulraj established New Calvary Ministries. Starting in a modest building with a small congregation, the church quickly grew in both size and influence. This growth is attributed to Paulraj's passionate preaching and unwavering dedication to his community. Pastor Paulraj's vision was to create a church that nurtured spiritual growth while also actively addressing the social and economic needs of the community. Under his leadership, New Calvary Ministries has become a beacon of hope and support, significantly impacting many lives.
